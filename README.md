# Red Ball Maze

A 2D top-down action-puzzle game built in Unity. Navigate through a maze, collect stars, and avoid the "Laser Killer" to escape the maze.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay
You control a **Red Ball** trapped in a grid of blocks. To win the game, you must explore the maze and collect all the stars to unlock a hidden key. Only after collecting the key will the final section of the maze unlock, allowing you to reach the goal.

### ⚠️ The Hazard
*   **The Laser Killer:** Stay away from the laser! Contact with a laser results in an immediate game over. Timing and precision are your best friends.

---

## 🕹️ Controls
Choose the control scheme that fits your playstyle:

| Action | Keyboard Input |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Move Up** | `W` or `↑ Up Arrow` |
| **Move Down** | `S` or `↓ Down Arrow` |
| **Move Left** | `A` or `← Left Arrow` |
| **Move Right** | `D` or `→ Right Arrow` |
